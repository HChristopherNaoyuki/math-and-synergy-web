About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Maria R. Chenault and Yukihiro Tashiro, Math and Language Synergy began with a vision to bridge the gap between language and mathematics education, empowering learners to succeed in both domains.

Our founders, seasoned educators with deep passion for languages and mathematics, recognized the rising need for an integrated approach to learning in a globalized world.

Our Mission

To offer integrated education that provides students with both the math and language skills needed for proficiency and success in a diverse, global world.

Our Vision

To create a world where language and mathematics complement each other, enabling students to excel academically and thrive in international environments.
