## About Us

Founded in 2010 by Maria R. Chenault and Yukihiro Tashiro, our institute began 
with a vision to bridge the gap between language and mathematics education. Our 
founders, seasoned educators, believed that proficiency in English and Japanese, 
combined with strong mathematical skills, opens global opportunities.

**A Vision of Holistic Learning:** We created a curriculum that highlights the connections between subjects, developing both language proficiency and analytical thinking in a supportive environment.

**Building a Community:** We have grown into a lively community of learners from all backgrounds, united by goals of mastering languages, excelling in math, or integrating both for academic and professional journeys.

**Innovative Programs and Experienced Faculty:** Our programs are dynamic, flexible, and tailored to student needs. Our faculty includes veteran language instructors and accomplished mathematicians passionate about student success.
